Privacy Policy

Your clipboard stays on your device.

Last updated: February 4, 2026.

ClippyDo is a local first clipboard manager for Windows. Clipboard contents never leave your device. Optional telemetry, if enabled, is anonymized and used only to understand feature usage and diagnose errors.

Information we collect

  • Clipboard history and related metadata stored locally on your device.
  • Optional anonymized telemetry about feature usage and application errors.

Information we do not collect

  • Clipboard content is never transmitted anywhere.
  • No personally identifying information is collected in telemetry.
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties.

How we use data

  • Local clipboard history enables search, previews, filters, notes, and comparisons.
  • Anonymized telemetry helps understand which features are most used and whether there are errors in code.

Sharing and disclosure

ClippyDo does not share clipboard contents or telemetry with third parties. We do not sell personal data. We only disclose information if required to comply with applicable law.

Storage and retention

Clipboard history is stored locally on your device. You can clear it at any time by deleting items or removing the local data store. Telemetry, when enabled, is anonymized and retained only as long as needed for diagnostics and product improvement.

Security

ClippyDo keeps clipboard data on device. We follow industry standard practices to reduce the risk of unintended access, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You can disable telemetry in the app settings. You can delete clipboard history at any time. Uninstalling the app removes local application data.

Children's privacy

ClippyDo is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated policy will be published on this page with a new revision date.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, please use the support contact listed on the Microsoft Store listing for ClippyDo.
